Ping Pong Project

Enter a number and click the Let's Ping Pong! button.

  1. It will replace numbers in the list divisible by 3 with the word "ping".
  2. It will replace numbers in the list divisible by 5 with the word "pong".
  3. It will replace numbers in the list divisible by 15 with the words "ping-pong".
